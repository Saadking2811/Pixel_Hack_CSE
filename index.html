About Pixel Hack
Overview
The CSE PIXEL HACK is an exciting competition where participants can express their creativity and coding skills by designing video games or simulations. The theme for this event could be something inspiring, such as "eco-friendly cities" or "space exploration." The Game Jam aims to bring together students with a shared passion for game development and innovation
Workshops
To help participants succeed, we will host workshops and mentorship sessions led by experienced game developers. These will cover the basics of game development, platform-specific tips, and best practices.
Event-Format
- Teams will be given the theme at the start of the event.
- Each team will have a set amount of time (e.g., 24-48 hours) to conceptualize, design, and develop their game.
- Judges will evaluate the games based on creativity, functionality, and alignment with the theme.
Goals
Participants will create simple, engaging games or simulations based on a specific theme. This event encourages creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills as teams work to develop games from scratch within a limited time frame.
